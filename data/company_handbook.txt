# Company Employee Handbook

## Welcome to TechCorp

TechCorp is a leading software development company established in 2015, specializing in cloud solutions, AI integration, and enterprise software development. We pride ourselves on innovation, employee well-being, and delivering cutting-edge technology solutions to our clients.

## About TechCorp

**Headquarters**: San Francisco, California
**Founded**: 2015
**Employees**: 500+
**Specializations**: Cloud Computing, Machine Learning, Enterprise Software

### Our Mission
To empower businesses through innovative technology solutions that drive growth and transformation.

### Our Values
- Innovation: We embrace new ideas and continuous improvement
- Integrity: We conduct business ethically and transparently
- Collaboration: We work together to achieve common goals
- Excellence: We strive for the highest quality in everything we do
- Customer Focus: We put our clients' needs first

## Work Hours and Flexibility

### Standard Hours
- Standard work week: Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM
- Lunch break: 1 hour (flexible timing)
- Core hours: 10 AM to 3 PM (when most team members are expected to be present)

### Flexible Work Arrangements
- Remote work: Allowed up to 3 days per week
- Flexible hours: With manager approval, 7 AM to 7 PM window
- Compressed week: 4 days, 10 hours per day (requires approval)

## Vacation and Time Off

### Paid Time Off
- New employees (0-2 years): 15 days per year
- Experienced employees (2-5 years): 20 days per year
- Senior employees (5+ years): 25 days per year
- All employees receive 10 company holidays per year

### Sick Leave
- 10 days per year for personal illness or medical appointments
- Additional days for family care (up to 5 days per year)

### Parental Leave
- 12 weeks paid parental leave for primary caregiver
- 4 weeks paid parental leave for secondary caregiver
- Job protection and benefits continuation during leave

## Compensation and Benefits

### Salary Review Process
- Annual reviews in March
- Performance-based adjustments (0-6% range)
- Merit increases based on role expansion and contributions
- Market-rate adjustments every 2 years

### Health Benefits
- Medical insurance (80% company-paid)
- Dental insurance (85% company-paid)
- Vision insurance (75% company-paid)
- Mental health support and counseling services
- Gym membership reimbursement ($50/month)

### Retirement Plans
- 401(k) with 4% company match
- Company-sponsored ESOP (Employee Stock Ownership Plan)
- Vesting period: 4 years with 1-year cliff

### Other Benefits
- Life insurance: 2x annual salary (company-paid)
- Disability insurance: 60% of salary (company-paid)
- Commuter benefits: $150/month pre-tax
- Parental leave: As detailed above

## Professional Development

### Training Budget
- Annual training budget: $2,000 per employee
- Covers certifications, courses, conferences, workshops
- Manager approval required for expenses over $500

### Career Development Program
- Annual career development planning sessions
- Mentorship program pairing senior and junior employees
- Leadership development programs for high-potential employees
- Tuition reimbursement: up to $5,000 per year for job-related degrees

### Conference Attendance
- Company sponsors 1-2 industry conferences per year per employee
- All-expense covered (registration, travel, accommodation)
- Internal knowledge sharing sessions upon return

## Office Policies

### Dress Code
- Business casual Monday-Thursday
- Casual Friday (jeans acceptable)
- Client meetings: Business attire recommended
- Engineering teams: Casual (jeans and TechCorp branded apparel)

### Equipment and Technology
- Laptop: Company-provided, refreshed every 3 years
- Monitor: Dual monitors for development roles
- Phone: Company phone or phone stipend ($50/month)
- Software licenses: Provided for job-related tools

### Office Amenities
- Free coffee and tea
- Snack room stocked daily
- Gym facilities on 3rd floor
- Collaboration spaces and meeting rooms
- Rooftop garden area

## Performance Management

### Annual Performance Review
- Self-assessment required
- Manager rating on 5-point scale
- 360-degree feedback from peers and team members
- Performance improvement plans (PIP) for underperformance
- Review cycle: January-March

### Goals and Objectives
- OKR (Objectives and Key Results) framework
- Quarterly goal setting and mid-year check-ins
- Manager-employee regular one-on-ones
- Performance metrics tracked in HubSpot

## Code of Conduct

### Expected Behavior
- Treat all employees with respect and dignity
- Maintain professional communication
- Arrive on time to meetings
- Complete assigned work by deadlines
- Report safety hazards immediately

### Prohibited Conduct
- Discrimination or harassment
- Misuse of company equipment
- Sharing confidential information
- Conflict of interest without disclosure
- Substance abuse at work

### Social Media Policy
- Represent the company professionally online
- Do not share company secrets or client information
- Include disclaimer when discussing work matters
- Company social media accounts managed by Marketing only

## Data Security and Confidentiality

### Confidential Information
- All client data is confidential and proprietary
- Non-disclosure agreements (NDA) required for all employees
- Violation can result in legal action and termination
- Confidentiality obligations extend 2 years after employment

### Password Policy
- Minimum 12 characters with mixed case and numbers
- Changed every 90 days
- Never share passwords with colleagues
- Two-factor authentication required for all accounts

### Data Access Controls
- Access only to systems required for your role
- Encryption required for all data transfers
- VPN required for remote work
- Regular security training mandatory

## Remote Work Guidelines

### Equipment at Home
- Company-provided laptop must be used
- Secure internet connection required
- Separate workspace preferred
- Company provides desk/chair stipend up to $500

### Communication
- Respond to messages within 2 business hours
- Maintain regular check-ins with manager
- Join all scheduled meetings
- Slack status should reflect availability

### Work Environment
- Quiet, professional background for video calls
- No confidential information displayed visibly
- Secure documents after use
- Regular breaks recommended

## Termination and Separation

### Notice Period
- Employees: 2 weeks notice
- Managers: 4 weeks notice
- Directors: 6 weeks notice
- Company may ask for immediate departure

### Final Paycheck
- Includes accrued PTO payout
- Processed within 5 business days
- COBRA benefits information provided
- Reference letters available upon request

## Complaints and Grievances

### Reporting Channels
1. Direct manager (preferred first step)
2. HR department
3. Anonymous hotline: 1-800-TECHCORP-1
4. Executive leadership

### Investigation Process
- Confidentiality maintained throughout
- No retaliation for good-faith complaints
- Investigation typically completed within 30 days
- Follow-up meeting with complainant
